## PROFILE OPTIMIZATION TIPS

1. **First Two Sentences Matter Most** – These appear in search results. Lead with your value proposition.

2. **Use Keywords Naturally** – Include: AI, automation, chatbot, integration, OpenAI, ChatGPT, Claude, Zapier, Make, Python, JavaScript, API. But write for humans, not just algorithms.

3. **Show Results, Not Just Skills** – "Reduced support tickets by 50%" beats "Expert in chatbot development."

4. **Specialize** – Being an "AI automation specialist" converts better than "full-stack developer who also does AI."

5. **Update Regularly** – Add new skills, technologies, and projects as you complete them.

6. **Profile Photo** – Professional, friendly, good lighting. No sunglasses, no group photos.

7. **Availability Badge** – Keep it updated. Clients filter by availability.

8. **Response Time** – Respond within 1 hour during business hours for best search ranking.
