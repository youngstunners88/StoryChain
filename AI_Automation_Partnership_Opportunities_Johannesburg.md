## Notes on Johannesburg Market

1. **Business Culture:** Johannesburg is South Africa's economic hub with a professional, relationship-driven business culture. In-person meetings are valued—consider face-to-face for key partnerships.

2. **Loadshedding Awareness:** South Africa experiences scheduled power outages. Position AI automation as helping businesses operate efficiently despite infrastructure challenges.

3. **Currency:** South African Rand (ZAR). Consider pricing in ZAR for local clients, USD for enterprise/multinational clients.

4. **Languages:** English is the primary business language in Johannesburg. No translation needed.

5. **Networking:** Key business areas include Sandton (financial district), Rosebank (upscale commercial), and Midrand (growing tech hub). Attend events in these areas.

6. **Timing:** Best outreach timing is Tuesday-Thursday mornings (South Africa time, SAST = UTC+2).
