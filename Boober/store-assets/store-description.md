## Full Description (4000 chars max)

**Boober - Your Trusted Taxi Companion in South Africa** 🇿🇦

Navigating South Africa's taxi system just got easier, safer, and more connected. Boober connects passengers with verified taxi drivers and real-time rank information - no payment integration needed, just the info you need for a safer journey.

---

### 🚕 KEY FEATURES

**Real-Time Rank Status**
- Check taxi queue capacity before you arrive
- Know which ranks are moving, full, or empty
- Marshal-reported updates for accuracy

**Verified Drivers**
- All drivers go through community verification
- Rate and review your rides
- Build trust through transparency

**Safety First**
- Share your trip in real-time with loved ones
- Emergency contact integration
- Verified driver profiles with photos

**Community-Powered**
- Marshals report queue status
- Drivers verify each other
- Passengers help improve routes

**No Payment Hassles**
- Cash-based system (no cards needed)
- Focus on information, not transactions
- Works with how taxis already operate

---

### 🎯 WHO IS BOOBER FOR?

**Passengers**
- Find the nearest rank with available taxis
- See queue wait times before you go
- Rate drivers and improve the community
- Share your location for safety

**Drivers**
- Get verified by fellow drivers
- Build your reputation
- Connect with passengers looking for rides
- Report rank conditions

**Marshals**
- Manage your rank's status
- Verify drivers
- Keep the community informed
- Earn recognition for your work

---

### 🛡️ SAFETY FEATURES

- **Trip Sharing**: Let family track your journey
- **Driver Verification**: See driver ratings before accepting
- **Community Reporting**: Flag safety concerns
- **Emergency Contacts**: Quick access when needed

---

### 🌍 DESIGNED FOR SOUTH AFRICA

Boober is built with South African commuters in mind:
- Works with existing taxi associations
- Supports local minibus taxi culture
- No disruption to current operations
- Enhances what already works

---

### 📱 HOW IT WORKS

1. **Download & Register** - Quick signup with phone or email
2. **Find Your Route** - See nearby ranks and availability
3. **Hail Your Ride** - Ping drivers or head to the rank
4. **Rate & Review** - Help improve the community

---

### 💡 IMPORTANT NOTES

- Location services required for full features
- Internet connection needed for real-time updates
- Works best in areas with active marshal participation
- Free to use - no hidden fees
