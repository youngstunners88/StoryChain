# Boober Press Release

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

## Boober Launches South Africa's First Comprehensive Taxi Network App

*Johannesburg, South Africa — March 1, 2026* — Boober today announced the launch of South Africa's most comprehensive taxi network app, designed to connect passengers with verified minibus taxi drivers across the country.

### Revolutionizing South African Transportation

Boober brings modern technology to the heart of South Africa's transportation ecosystem, offering:

- **Verified Drivers**: Every driver undergoes rigorous background checks and vehicle verification
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Passengers can track their ride in real-time for added peace of mind
- **Fair Pricing**: Transparent fares based on distance and route
- **Multiple Roles**: Separate interfaces for Passengers, Drivers, and Taxi Rank Marshals
- **Community Focus**: Built with input from taxi industry stakeholders

### Quote from the Founder

"We're not here to replace the taxi industry—we're here to enhance it," said the Boober team. "South Africans have been using minibus taxis for decades, and Boober respects that tradition while making it safer and more accessible for everyone."

### Key Features

1. **Passenger App**: Book rides, track drivers, rate experiences
2. **Driver App**: Accept bookings, manage routes, track earnings
3. **Marshal App**: Manage taxi queues, coordinate pick-ups
4. **Safety First**: Driver verification, emergency contacts, trip sharing
5. **Earn Rewards**: Referral bonuses, daily streaks, leaderboards
