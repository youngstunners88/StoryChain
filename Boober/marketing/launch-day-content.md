**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Boober Launches South Africa's First Safety-Verified Minibus Taxi App**

*Johannesburg, 28 February 2026* — Boober, South Africa's newest taxi booking platform, today launched its mobile app designed to bring safety, transparency, and reliability to the country's iconic minibus taxi industry.

### What is Boober?

Boober is a mobile application that connects passengers with verified minibus taxi drivers. Unlike traditional taxi hailing, Boober provides:

- **Driver Verification**: Every driver is background-checked and verified before joining the platform
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Passengers can share their trip with family for added safety
- **Transparent Pricing**: No hidden fees or surprise charges
- **Instant Payments**: Drivers receive payment immediately after each trip

### Why Boober Matters

"For millions of South Africans, minibus taxis are more than transport — they're the backbone of our communities," said the Boober team. "We're not replacing the taxi industry; we're working with it. Boober respects the existing ecosystem while giving passengers and drivers new tools to stay safe and connected."

### Key Features

1. **Verified Driver Profiles**: View your driver's photo, name, vehicle details, and verification status before you hop in
2. **Share My Trip**: Send your trip details to family or friends with one tap
3. **Real-Time ETA**: Know exactly when your taxi will arrive
4. **Driver Ratings**: Community-driven quality assurance
5. **Instant Payouts**: Drivers get paid within minutes of completing trips

### Availability

Boober is available for download on the **App Store** and **Google Play Store** starting today. The app is currently operational in Johannesburg and surrounding areas, with plans to expand to other major South African cities.

### Quote for Media

> "Boober is about trust. When you book a Boober taxi, you know exactly who you're getting — their name, their face, their vehicle. That's peace of mind every passenger deserves."
