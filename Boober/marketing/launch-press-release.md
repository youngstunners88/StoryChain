# Boober Launch Day Press Release

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Boober Launches in South Africa to Deliver Safer, Verified Taxi Rides**
Johannesburg, 28 February 2026 – Boober, the new South African taxi app, launches today with a promise to make everyday journeys safer, friendlier, and more transparent for everyone who rides and drives within the minibus taxi ecosystem.

Boober pairs deeply local knowledge of South African taxis with modern safety science. Every driver who joins the platform is verified through ID checks, vehicle inspections, and behaviour training. Passengers receive a full driver profile, real-time location tracking, and a one-tap emergency contact, all through a polished mobile experience.

“South African taxis carry the pulse of the nation, but they deserve technology that protects their drivers and riders,” said [Founder Name], Boober CEO. “Today we launch as partners to that ecosystem, not disruptors—working with associations, marshals, and communities to build trust, highlight professionalism, and reward safe behaviour.”

Key features:
- Driver verification and community-led screening
- Transparent pricing with fare estimates before booking
- Safety-first tools: Live trip tracking, share ride, emergency SOS
- Community scorecards for passengers and drivers
- Payments that respect how South Africans pay for taxis today

Boober is available on Android and iOS in Johannesburg, Pretoria, Cape Town, and Durban. Riders can download the app, browse verified drivers, and hail rides in under 60 seconds. Drivers can apply through Boober’s in-app onboarding, get payments settled quickly, and earn safety bonuses for consistent behaviour.

Boober partners with existing taxi associations, corporates, and neighbourhood marshals to keep dispatch and marshal teams in the loop—ensuring rapid response and local accountability.

For more information, visit [https://boober.app](https://boober.app) or contact press@boober.app.
