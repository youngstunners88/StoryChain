### Content Style Guide

- **Aspect ratio**: 9:16 (vertical, full screen)
- **Duration**: 15-30 seconds for launch week
- **Text overlay**: White, bold, 30% from top
- **CTA**: "Download Boober on the App Store" or "Search Boober on Google Play"
- **Language**: Mix English + Zulu/Sotho for authenticity
