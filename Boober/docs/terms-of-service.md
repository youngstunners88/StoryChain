## 4. Driver Verification Requirements

### 4.1 Eligibility

All drivers on the Boober platform must meet the following requirements:

- Valid South African driver's license (Code B or higher)
- Professional driving permit (PDP) for commercial driving
- Valid taxi operating license or permit
- Vehicle roadworthy certificate
- Minimum 2 years of driving experience
- Age 21 years or older
- Clear criminal record (background check required)
- No more than 3 demerit points on license

### 4.2 Vehicle Requirements

- Vehicle must be roadworthy and regularly inspected
- Valid motor vehicle insurance (comprehensive or third-party)
- Vehicle must meet emission standards
- Maximum vehicle age: 15 years
- Regular maintenance and cleanliness standards

### 4.3 Verification Process

Drivers must submit:
1. Identity document (SA ID or passport)
2. Driver's license with PDP
3. Taxi operating license
4. Vehicle registration documents
5. Insurance certificate
6. Proof of roadworthy certificate
7. Criminal background check results

All documents are verified through relevant South African authorities.

### 4.4 Ongoing Compliance

Drivers must:
- Maintain valid licenses and permits at all times
- Notify Boober within 48 hours of any license suspension or violations
- Submit to periodic re-verification
- Complete safety training modules
