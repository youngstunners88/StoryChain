# Boober Driver Onboarding Guide

Thank you for your interest in becoming a Boober driver! This guide walks you through the registration and verification process.

## 1. Driver Requirements

Before applying, ensure you meet these requirements:

### Personal Requirements
- South African citizen or legal resident
- Age 21 years or older
- Valid South African ID or valid passport with work permit
- Clear criminal record
- No more than 3 demerit points on driver's license

### License Requirements
- Valid driver's license (Code B minimum)
- Professional Driving Permit (PDP) - valid and current
- Taxi operating license from your local taxi board

### Vehicle Requirements
- Vehicle registered in your name or company name
- Valid roadworthy certificate
- Comprehensive insurance or third-party insurance
- Vehicle age: 15 years or newer
- Passenger capacity: 4-22 passengers

## 2. Registration Process

### Step 1: Create Driver Account
1. Download the Boober Driver App from Play Store
2. Enter your mobile number
3. Verify via SMS code
4. Create your password

### Step 2: Submit Personal Information
1. Full legal name (matching ID)
2. Email address
3. Physical residential address
4. Upload clear photos of:
   - ID document (both sides)
   - Driver's license (front and back)
   - PDP card
   - Proof of taxi operating license

### Step 3: Vehicle Information
1. Vehicle make, model, and year
2. License plate number
3. Upload photos of:
   - Vehicle registration document
   - Insurance certificate
   - Roadworthy certificate
   - Vehicle (front, back, interior)

### Step 4: Background Check
1. We conduct verification checks:
   - Criminal record check
   - License validity verification
   - Vehicle status check
2. This typically takes 3-5 business days
3. You'll receive an email when verified

### Step 5: Training
1. Complete online safety training (approx. 30 minutes)
2. Pass the assessment quiz (80% required)
3. Watch orientation videos

### Step 6: Vehicle Inspection
1. Schedule an inspection appointment
2. Bring all vehicle documents
3. Our team inspects your vehicle
4. Approve or provide remediation list

### Step 7: Go Online
Once all checks pass, you're ready to accept rides!

## 3. Getting Started

### Understanding the Driver App

**Main Screen:**
- Online/Offline toggle
- Current location map
- Today's earnings
- Rating display

**Accepting Rides:**
- Tap to see ride details
- Accept or decline within 15 seconds
- Navigate to pickup

**During Ride:**
- Start trip when passenger boards
- Follow GPS route
- End trip at destination
- Collect fare (if cash)

### Fare Structure

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Base fare | R15.00 (varies by city) |
| Per km | R8.00 per kilometre |
| Per minute | R1.50 per minute |
| Minimum fare | R35.00 |
| Peak surcharge | 1.5x during high demand |

### Earnings and Payments

**How You Get Paid:**
- Bank transfers every Tuesday
- Minimum R100 for payout
- Track earnings in the app

**Driver Incentives:**
- Weekly bonuses for top ratings
- Peak hour incentives
- Refer-a-driver bonuses

## 4. Best Practices

### Customer Service
- Greet passengers warmly
- Help with luggage
- Maintain conversation if passenger initiates
- Play neutral music unless asked otherwise

### Safety
- Always wear your seatbelt
- Follow traffic laws
- Never use phone while passengers are in vehicle
- Maintain professional demeanor

### Navigation
- Use in-app GPS navigation
- Confirm destination with passenger
- Ask preferred route if unsure
- Inform passenger of delays

### Vehicle Standards
- Keep vehicle clean inside and out
- Ensure air conditioning works
- Remove personal items from view
- No smoking in vehicle

## 5. Common Issues

### Verification Delays
- Ensure documents are clear and not expired
- Check that name matches across all documents
- Respond promptly to any additional requests

### Account Holds
- May occur for: safety reports, unusual activity, document expiry
- Contact support to resolve

### Low Ratings
- Common causes: route issues, vehicle cleanliness, communication
- Review passenger feedback
- Improve in identified areas
