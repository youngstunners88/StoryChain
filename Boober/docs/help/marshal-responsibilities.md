# Boober Marshal Responsibilities Guide

Welcome to the Boober Marshal team! This guide outlines your duties and responsibilities as a rank marshal.

## 1. Role Overview

Marshals are essential to Boober's operations, managing taxi ranks and ensuring efficient service for both drivers and passengers.

**Key Objectives:**
- Maintain orderly queue systems
- Facilitate smooth passenger boarding
- Report accurate rank capacity data
- Ensure safety and security at ranks
- Coordinate with local stakeholders

## 2. Daily Responsibilities

### 2.1 Queue Management

**Before Shift:**
- Arrive 15 minutes before scheduled start
- Check in via the Marshal App
- Review any overnight updates

**During Shift:**
- Organize taxi queue according to arrival time
- Ensure fair and orderly boarding
- Manage passenger flow
- Handle queue jumping or disputes

**End of Shift:**
- Complete shift report
- Hand over to incoming marshal
- Report any ongoing issues

### 2.2 Rank Capacity Reporting

**Why It Matters:**
Accurate capacity data helps Boober:
- Match ride requests to available drivers
- Predict wait times for passengers
- Optimize driver dispatch

**How to Report:**
1. Open Marshal App
2. Tap "Update Capacity"
3. Select:
   - Current queue length (number of taxis)
   - Estimated wait time
   - Special conditions (events, weather, etc.)
4. Submit

**Reporting Frequency:**
- Every 30 minutes minimum
- Immediately after significant changes
- Before leaving shift

### 2.3 Passenger Assistance

- Direct passengers to correct taxi
- Help with large luggage
- Provide information on routes and fares
- Assist elderly or disabled passengers
- Handle complaints professionally

## 3. Safety Protocols

### 3.1 Personal Safety

- Always wear your Boober marshal vest
- Stay in well-lit, visible areas
- Never confront aggressive individuals
- Call security or police if needed
- Report any threats immediately

### 3.2 Passenger Safety

- Ensure passengers board safely
- Verify taxi legitimacy (license, signage)
- Report suspicious vehicles
- Keep rank area clear of hazards

### 3.3 Driver Safety

- Treat all drivers professionally
- Resolve disputes calmly
- Report driver violations to Boober
- Ensure drivers have safe access to queue

## 4. Conflict Resolution

### 4.1 Common Issues

| Issue | Resolution Approach |
|-------|---------------------|
| Queue jumping | Firm but polite redirection; remind of rules |
| Passenger-driver dispute | Listen to both sides; mediate fairly |
| Driver disputes | Review queue order; enforce rules consistently |
| Aggressive behavior | Stay calm; do not engage; call security |

### 4.2 De-escalation Techniques

1. **Listen:** Let parties explain their concerns
2. **Acknowledge:** Show you understand
3. **Find common ground:** Focus on shared interests
4. **Offer solutions:** Propose fair compromises
5. **Escalate if needed:** Know when to involve authorities

### 4.3 When to Escalate

Contact Boober support or emergency services if:
- Physical altercation occurs
- Someone threatens violence
- Illegal activity is observed
- Medical emergency happens
- Situation exceeds your authority

## 5. Working with Taxi Associations

### 5.1 Building Relationships

- Introduce yourself to local association leaders
- Understand their processes and concerns
- Communicate professionally at all times
- Respect existing rank traditions

### 5.2 Collaboration

- Share relevant information (capacity, issues)
- Coordinate during peak times or events
- Address concerns promptly
- Maintain neutrality in internal disputes

### 5.3 Handling Tensions

- Stay neutral in association disputes
- Focus on passenger service
- Don't take sides in political disputes
- Report significant issues to Boober

## 6. Reporting Requirements

### 6.1 Daily Reports

Submit via Marshal App:
- Hours worked
- Number of taxis processed
- Number of passengers assisted
- Any incidents

### 6.2 Incident Reports

Report immediately:
- Accidents
- Assaults or threats
- Theft or vandalism
- Medical emergencies
- Police involvement

### 6.3 Feedback

- Suggest operational improvements
- Report infrastructure issues
- Share passenger/driver feedback
- Recommend training topics

## 7. Marshal App Features

### Getting Started
1. Download from Play Store
2. Log in with your credentials
3. Enable notifications
4. Complete profile setup

### Key Features
- **Shift Check-in/out:** Track your hours
- **Capacity Update:** Quick status reporting
- **Issue Reporting:** Submit incidents
- **Earnings:** View your payment
- **Schedule:** View upcoming shifts
- **Messages:** Receive Boober updates

## 8. Code of Conduct

### You Must:
- Arrive on time and prepared
- Wear marshal identification at all times
- Follow Boober procedures
- Treat everyone with respect
- Maintain professional appearance
- Keep personal information confidential

### You Must Not:
- Accept bribes or gratuities
- Favour specific drivers or passengers
- Share passenger information
- Engage in illegal activity
- Use profanity or aggressive language
- Leave shift without proper handover
