# Boober Getting Started Guide

Welcome to Boober! This guide will help you get up and running quickly.

## 1. Downloading and Installing

### For Android
1. Open Google Play Store on your device
2. Search for "Boober"
3. Tap "Install"
4. Open the app once installed

### For iOS
1. Open App Store on your device
2. Search for "Boober"
3. Tap "Get"
4. Open the app once installed

## 2. Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Phone Verification
1. Open the Boober app
2. Enter your South African mobile number
3. You'll receive an SMS with a verification code
4. Enter the code to verify your number

### Step 2: Basic Information
1. Enter your full name (as it appears on your ID)
2. Add your email address
3. Create a secure password
4. Add a profile photo (optional but recommended)

### Step 3: Payment Setup
1. Add your preferred payment method
2. We accept:
   - Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
   - Cash on delivery
   - Mobile money (coming soon)
3. Your payment information is encrypted and secure

## 3. Booking Your First Ride

### Setting Your Pickup Location
1. The app automatically detects your current location
2. Or tap "Enter pickup location" to type an address
3. You can save home and work locations for quick access

### Choosing Your Destination
1. Type your destination in the search bar
2. Select from the suggested locations
3. You can also tap on the map to select a location

### Confirming Your Ride
1. Review the estimated fare
2. Select your preferred ride type:
   - **Standard:** Affordable shared or solo options
   - **Premium:** Higher-end vehicles
3. Tap "Book Ride"
4. Wait for a driver to accept

### During Your Ride
- Track your driver's location on the map
- Your driver's name and photo are displayed
- Contact your driver through the in-app chat
- Share your trip status with family/friends

### Arriving at Destination
1. Your driver will take you to the entered address
2. Payment is processed automatically
3. Rate your driver (optional but appreciated)
4. You're done!

## 4. Key Features

### Saved Locations
- Add your home, work, and favorite places
- One-tap booking from saved locations

### Trip History
- View all your past trips
- Download receipts for business travel
- Track your spending

### Share Trip
- Share your live location with contacts
- Send estimated arrival time

### Multiple Stops
- Add multiple destinations
- Perfect for running errands

## 5. Tips for a Great Experience

- **Be ready:** Be at the pickup location when your driver arrives
- **Verify your driver:** Check the license plate and driver photo
- **Communication:** Use in-app chat for directions
- **Rating:** Rate your drivers to maintain service quality

## 6. Need Help?

- **FAQ:** Check our Help section for common questions
- **Support:** Email support@boober.co.za
- **Emergencies:** Contact emergency services (10111) for safety emergencies
