# Boober Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Boober?
Boober is a ride-hailing platform connecting passengers with licensed taxi drivers in South Africa. We provide a safe, reliable, and convenient way to book rides through our mobile app.

### Is Boober available in my area?
Boober currently operates in major South African cities including Johannesburg, Pretoria, Durban, Cape Town, and Port Elizabeth. We're continuously expanding to new areas.

### How much does a ride cost?
Fare calculation includes:
- Base fare: R15.00
- Per kilometre: R8.00
- Per minute: R1.50
- Minimum fare: R35.00

Peak surcharges may apply during high-demand periods. The exact fare is displayed before you confirm your ride.

## Account and Registration

### How do I create an account?
1. Download the Boober app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store
2. Enter your mobile number
3. Verify with the SMS code sent to your phone
4. Complete your profile with name and email

### Is there an age requirement?
Yes. Users must be 18 years or older. Users under 18 may only travel when accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.

### Can I use Boober without a smartphone?
No, Boober requires our mobile app to book and manage rides.

## Booking a Ride

### How do I book a ride?
1. Open the app and allow location access
2. Enter your destination
3. Review the fare estimate
4. Tap "Book Ride"
5. Wait for a driver to accept
6. Track your driver on the map

### Can I book a ride for someone else?
Yes. During booking, you can enter the passenger's phone number so they receive trip updates. The trip will still charge to your account.

### Can I schedule a ride in advance?
Yes. You can schedule rides up to 7 days in advance. Look for the "Schedule" button on the booking screen.

### What if I need to make multiple stops?
You can add multiple stops during booking. Tap "Add Stop" and enter each destination in order.

### How do I cancel a ride?
- **Before driver accepts:** Free cancellation
- **After driver accepts:** Tap "Cancel" in the app
- Cancellation fees may apply for frequent cancellations

## During the Ride

### How do I contact my driver?
Use the in-app chat or call feature. Your phone number is hidden from the driver for your privacy.

### Can I request a specific driver?
No. Ride assignments are automated based on proximity and availability.

### What if I left something in the vehicle?
Contact the driver through the app immediately. If the item isn't found, submit a lost item report through Support.

### Can I change my destination during a trip?
Yes. Inform your driver and the app will update the route and fare accordingly.

### What happens if my driver takes a wrong route?
The app tracks the route. If you believe an incorrect route was taken, you can dispute the fare after the trip.

## Payment

### What payment methods are accepted?
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- Cash on delivery
- Mobile money (coming soon)

### When am I charged?
Payment is processed automatically after your trip ends.

### Can I get a receipt?
Yes. Trip receipts are available in your app under "Trip History." You can email receipts to yourself for business purposes.

### How do I add or remove payment methods?
Go to Settings > Payment Methods in the app to manage your cards.

### What is the cancellation fee?
Free cancellation within 2 minutes of driver acceptance or more than 5 minutes before arrival. Frequent cancellations (3+ in 24 hours) may incur a small fee.

## Safety

### Is Boober safe?
We prioritize safety by:
- Verifying all drivers through background checks
- Tracking rides in real-time
- Allowing you to share trip status
- Providing in-app emergency assistance

### What should I do in an emergency?
- Contact emergency services: 10111 (police), 10177 (ambulance)
- Use the "Emergency" button in the app
- Share your live location with a trusted contact

### Can I see driver details before the ride?
Yes. The app displays:
- Driver name and photo
- Vehicle make, model, and color
- License plate number
- Rating

### What if I feel unsafe?
Trust your instincts. Ask the driver to stop in a safe, public location. Use the emergency button or call for help.

## Ratings and Feedback

### How do ratings work?
After each trip, you can rate your driver from 1-5 stars. Drivers also rate passengers. This helps maintain service quality.

### What if I had a bad experience?
- Rate your driver honestly
- Submit feedback through the app
- Contact support if the issue is serious

### Can I dispute a rating?
Yes. Contact support with details about your trip.

## Driver-Related Questions

### How are drivers verified?
All drivers must pass:
- Criminal background check
- License and permit verification
- Vehicle inspection
- Identity verification

### What if my driver behaves inappropriately?
Report the driver immediately through the app or by contacting support. We take all reports seriously and may suspend or remove drivers.

## Technical Issues

### The app isn't working. What should I do?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Update the app to the latest version
3. Restart your phone
4. Contact support if the issue persists

### Why isn't my location accurate?
- Enable location permissions for the app
- Ensure GPS is turned on
- Try opening the app again

### I didn't receive the SMS verification code.
- Check your phone number is correct
- Ensure you have network signal
- Try the "Resend Code" option

## Complaints and Disputes

### How do I file a complaint?
Email support@boober.co.za with:
- Your trip details
- Description of the issue
- Any supporting evidence

We aim to respond within 24 hours.

### What if I disagree with a fare?
Submit a fare dispute within 48 hours of your trip. We'll review GPS data and trip details.

### Can I get a refund?
Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis for valid issues such as:
- Incorrect fare calculation
- Driver no-show
- Service quality issues

## Privacy and Data

### What information does Boober collect?
We collect:
- Location data (for ride matching)
- Account information
- Trip history
- Device information

See our Privacy Policy for full details.

### Can I delete my data?
Yes. You can request data deletion through your account settings or by contacting privacy@boober.co.za.

### Is my location always tracked?
Location is tracked only:
- While booking a ride
- During an active trip
- If you enable background location for faster booking

You can disable location tracking in your device settings.
