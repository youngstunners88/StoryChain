# Boober Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 24 February 2026  
**Last Updated:** 24 February 2026

## 1. Introduction

Boober ("we," "us," or "our") is a ride-hailing platform connecting passengers with licensed taxi drivers in South Africa. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information:** Full name, phone number, email address, and profile photo
- **Payment Information:** Stored payment method details (processed securely through third-party providers)
- **User Profile:** Preferred pickup/dropoff locations, frequently used routes, and accessibility requirements

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Location Data:** Real-time GPS coordinates when the app is active, including:
  - Current location for ride matching
  - Pickup and dropoff addresses
  - Route history for trip records
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App interactions, ride history, search queries

### 2.3 Information from Other Sources

- Driver verification data from licensing authorities
- Background check results from accredited service providers

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Ride matching and dispatch | Performance of contract |
| Location tracking for safe pickups | Consent |
| Trip history and receipts | Performance of contract |
| Customer support | Legitimate interest |
| Safety and fraud prevention | Legal obligation |
| Service improvement and analytics | Legitimate interest |
| Communications regarding rides | Performance of contract |

## 4. Data Sharing

### 4.1 With Drivers

To facilitate ride matching, we share with drivers:
- Pickup location and destination
- Your first name and profile photo
- Trip details (estimated arrival, fare estimate)

**We do NOT share your phone number with drivers.** All communication occurs through the app.

### 4.2 With Third Parties

- **Payment Processors:** To process fare payments (we do not store card details)
- **Verification Services:** For driver background checks and vehicle verification
- **Legal Authorities:** When required by law or for safety purposes

### 4.3 Data We Do NOT Sell

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 5. User Rights (POPIA Compliance)

Under POPIA, you have the following rights:

### 5.1 Right to Access
You may request a copy of all personal information we hold about you.

### 5.2 Right to Correction
You may request correction of inaccurate or misleading personal information.

### 5.3 Right to Deletion
You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to:
- Legal retention requirements
- Ongoing disputes
- Fraud prevention requirements

### 5.4 Right to Data Portability
You may request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

### 5.5 Right to Object
You may object to processing based on legitimate interests.

### 5.6 Right to Withdraw Consent
For location tracking and other consent-based processing, you may withdraw consent at any time through app settings.

## 6. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account data | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Trip history | 5 years for tax/compliance |
| Location data | 12 months (anonymized after trip) |
| Device identifiers | Until consent withdrawn |

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Secure storage with encryption at rest
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits
- Employee training on data protection

## 8. Location Tracking Consent

### 8.1 When We Track Location
- **While booking a ride:** Required for pickup matching
- **During active trip:** Required for route navigation and safety
- **Background location:** Optional; enables faster booking

### 8.2 Managing Location Permissions
You can manage location permissions through:
- App settings (Settings > Privacy > Location)
- Device operating system settings

Withdrawing location consent may limit your ability to use certain features.

## 9. Age Restrictions

- **Minimum Age:** Users must be 18 years or older to create an account
- **Supervised Travel:** Users under 18 may only use the service when accompanied by a parent or legal guardian
- **Driver Age:** Drivers must be 21 years or older

## 10. User-Generated Content

### 10.1 Content You Provide
Reviews, ratings, and feedback you submit are public and associated with your account. You are responsible for ensuring your content does not:
- Defame or harass others
- Contain personal information about third parties
- Violate any laws or regulations

### 10.2 Our Rights
We reserve the right to remove content that violates these guidelines.

## 11. International Data Transfers

All data is stored on servers within South Africa. No personal information is transferred outside the country.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via:
- App notification
- Email (for significant changes)

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when changes were last made.
