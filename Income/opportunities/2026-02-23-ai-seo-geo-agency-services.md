## Opportunity Overview

Traditional SEO is evolving into Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and AI SEO. With ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and other AI search engines becoming primary discovery channels, businesses need specialists who understand how to appear in AI-generated answers—not just traditional search rankings.

- Forbes: "AI SEO is 2026's new king"
- Brands asking "How do we become the suggestion when an agent is asked to find the best option?"
- SEO agencies reporting significant revenue growth from AI visibility services
- Most businesses have zero AI visibility strategy
