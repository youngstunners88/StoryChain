## Executive Summary

Selling AI prompts and prompt packs is a low-barrier, scalable passive income opportunity. As AI adoption accelerates, there's growing demand for high-quality, tested prompts that help users get better results from ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, and other AI tools. Creators can earn $100-5,000+ per month selling prompt packs on marketplaces like PromptBase, Etsy, and Gumroad.

## Opportunity Overview

### Market Context
- PromptBase has 100,000+ prompts listed
- Top sellers earn $1,000-10,000/month
- Average prompt sells for $2-5, packs for $10-50
- Growing demand as AI tools proliferate
- Low competition in niche categories

1. Identify niche/need
2. Create 20-50 prompts

## Success Factors

### What Makes Prompts Sell

1. **Specificity:** "Instagram caption generator for fitness coaches" sells better than "social media prompts"
2. **Tested Quality:** Include example outputs in listings
3. **Clear Value Proposition:** Time saved, quality improvement
4. **Visual Appeal:** Professional thumbnails increase clicks
5. **Niche Focus:** Underserved verticals have less competition

### Pricing Strategy
