## Executive Summary

SaaS affiliate programs offer recurring commissions on software subscriptions, creating passive income streams that compound over time. Unlike traditional one-time affiliate sales, SaaS affiliates earn a percentage of monthly payments for as long as the customer remains subscribed. Top programs offer 20-40% recurring commissions, with some high-ticket options paying $1,000+ per sale.

### Market Context
- SaaS market projected to reach $307B by 2026
- 88% of organizations now use AI tools (McKinsey 2025)
- Average SaaS affiliate earns $500-5,000/month passively
- Top performers earn $10,000-50,000/month

## Revenue Potential

### Conservative Estimate
- **Time to First Dollar:** 2-4 weeks
- **Monthly Revenue (Month 6):** $200-500
- **Monthly Revenue (Year 1):** $1,000-3,000
- **Monthly Revenue (Year 2+):** $3,000-10,000+

### Realistic Scenarios

| Scenario | Referrals/Month | Avg. Commission | Monthly Revenue |
|----------|-----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Beginner | 5-10 | $50-100 | $250-1,000 |
| Intermediate | 20-50 | $75-150 | $1,500-7,500 |
| Advanced | 50-100+ | $100-200 | $5,000-20,000 |

### Compounding Effect
Recurring commissions compound as you add more customers:
- Month 1: 10 referrals × $50 = $500/month
- Month 6: 60 total referrals × $50 = $3,000/month
- Month 12: 120 total referrals × $50 = $6,000/month

## Required Effort & Resources

### Initial Setup (Week 1)
- **Time Investment:** 10-20 hours
- **Financial Investment:** $50-200 (website, hosting)
- **Skills Required:** Content creation, basic SEO

### Ongoing Maintenance
- **Time Investment:** 5-10 hours/week
- **Tools Needed:** Website/blog, email marketing (optional)

### Recommended Tools
- Website: WordPress, Webflow, or Carrd ($5-20/month)
- Analytics: Google Analytics (free)
- Email: ConvertKit, MailerLite (free tier available)
- SEO: Ahrefs, SEMrush, or free alternatives
