**Type:** Passive Income / Digital Products

## Overview

Selling digital templates (Notion, Google Sheets, Canva) has become a legitimate business model with documented cases of sellers earning $115K/month. The key is finding underserved niches and creating high-quality, problem-solving templates.

| Revenue Level | Monthly Earnings | Requirements |
|---------------|------------------|--------------|

## Success Factors

Based on analysis of top sellers:

1. **Niche Down** - "Budget tracker for freelancers" beats "Budget template"
2. **Solve Specific Problems** - Not just "organize your life" but "track client payments and taxes"
3. **Professional Presentation** - Mockups, video walkthroughs, clear descriptions
4. **Customer Support** - Quick responses, updates, tutorials
5. **Marketing** - Consistent social presence, SEO optimization

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Platform saturation | Differentiate with niche, quality, marketing |
| Copycats | Trademark brand, build community |
| Platform changes | Diversify across platforms, build email list |
| Price pressure | Add value through support, updates, bundles |
