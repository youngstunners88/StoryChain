## Overview

Crypto affiliate programs offer some of the highest commission rates in the affiliate marketing industry, ranging from 30% to 70% of trading fees. Unlike traditional affiliate programs that pay one-time commissions, many crypto programs offer lifetime recurring revenue from referred users' trading activity.

## Compliance & Best Practices

### Legal Requirements
1. **Always Disclose**
   - Clear affiliate disclosure on all content
   - FTC compliance (or local equivalent)
   - "This post contains affiliate links" statements

2. **Know Your Audience's Jurisdiction**
   - Some programs geo-restricted
   - KYC requirements vary
   - Tax obligations differ by country

### Best Practices
1. **Only Recommend What You Trust**
   - Use products yourself first
   - Research platform reputation
   - Consider user experience

2. **Provide Real Value**
   - Educational content first
   - Affiliate links second
   - Build trust for long-term success

3. **Track Everything**
   - Use unique links per platform
   - Monitor conversion rates
   - Optimize based on data
