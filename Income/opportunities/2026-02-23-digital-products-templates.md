## Opportunity Overview

Selling digital templates (Notion, Canva, AI prompts, spreadsheets) on platforms like Gumroad, Etsy, and Notion Marketplace represents a $0-startup, high-margin opportunity. Creators report $3,500/month from Notion templates alone. The self-improvement and productivity markets are particularly lucrative.

## Market Data

- **Template Market:** Notion marketplace has 10,000+ templates
- **Proven Success:** $3,500/month documented from Notion templates on Gumroad
- **Platform Fees:** Gumroad charges 10% + payment processing (no listing fees)
- **Target Markets:** Productivity enthusiasts, job seekers, small business owners, students
