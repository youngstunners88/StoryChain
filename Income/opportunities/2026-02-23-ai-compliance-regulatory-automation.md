## Opportunity Overview

New AI compliance laws are creating urgent demand for automation solutions. Small businesses worldwide are struggling with requirements like "AI Transparency Reports" and documentation mandates—but lack the expertise or tools to comply. This regulatory gap represents an immediate, high-value business opportunity.

- Small businesses need help with AI transparency reports, data governance, documentation
- Regulatory expertise gap = pricing power
