## Opportunity Overview

AI receptionist services represent one of the fastest-growing B2B opportunities in 2026. With Newo.ai raising $25M Series A and the AI voice automation market valued at $2.3 billion, this is a proven, high-demand sector. Small businesses lose an estimated $50K/year from missed calls alone—creating immediate ROI propositions.

## Competition Analysis

**Low-Medium Competition:**
- Most agencies focus on general AI automation
- Few specifically target voice AI for service businesses
- White label platforms make entry easy
- Local market knowledge is a moat

**Differentiation Strategies:**
- Specialise in one industry (e.g., dental offices only)
- Offer 24/7 support and custom voice training
- Bundle with other services (website, SEO)
- Provide detailed call analytics and reporting
