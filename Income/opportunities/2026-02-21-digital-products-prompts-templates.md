# AI-Generated Digital Products & Prompt Packs

**Type:** Passive Income / Digital Products  
**Date Identified:** 2026-02-21  
**Priority Score:** 7/10

---

## Overview

Digital products offer 70-90% profit margins with minimal ongoing maintenance. AI tools now enable rapid creation of templates, prompt packs, design assets, and educational content that can sell repeatedly with no inventory or fulfillment costs. [^1]

---

## Product Categories

### 1. Prompt Packs & Prompt Engineering
- **What:** Curated prompt collections for specific use cases
- **Price Point:** $9 - $97 per pack
- **Effort:** 5-10 hours to create
- **Examples:** ChatGPT prompts for business, Midjourney style guides, Claude writing prompts

### 2. AI Templates
- **What:** Ready-to-use templates for common tasks
- **Price Point:** $15 - $150
- **Effort:** 3-8 hours per template
- **Examples:** Email sequences, business plans, social media calendars, proposal templates

### 3. Design Assets
- **What:** AI-generated graphics, icons, backgrounds
- **Price Point:** $5 - $50 per asset or bundle
- **Effort:** 2-5 hours per bundle
- **Platforms:** Etsy, Creative Market, Gumroad

### 4. Educational Products
- **What:** Courses, guides, cheat sheets
- **Price Point:** $19 - $297
- **Effort:** 20-40 hours initial creation
- **Examples:** "AI for Small Business" guide, "Prompt Engineering Masterclass"

### 5. Micro-SaaS / API Products
- **What:** Simple AI-powered tools or APIs
- **Price Point:** $5 - $99/month subscription
- **Effort:** 40-100+ hours development
- **Examples:** AI writing assistant, image generator wrapper, chatbot builder

## Required Effort & Resources

### Initial Investment
- **Time:** 20-50 hours product creation per product line
- **Money:** $50 - $200 (platform fees, basic tools, graphics)
- **Skills:** Product design, AI prompting, basic marketing

### Ongoing Requirements
- 5-10 hours/month maintenance and updates
- Customer support (minimal for digital products)
- Marketing and content creation for traffic

### Platforms to Sell On
1. **Gumroad** - Best for prompt packs and guides (low fees)
2. **Etsy** - Great for design assets and templates
3. **Creative Market** - Professional design assets
4. **Payhip** - Simple storefront
5. **Own website** - Higher margins, more control

## Action Steps to Pursue

### Week 1: Research & Planning
1. Research best-selling digital products on target platforms
2. Identify 3-5 product ideas with proven demand
3. Choose primary platform(s) for launch
4. Study competitor products and pricing

### Week 2-3: Product Creation
1. Create first product (start with prompt pack - fastest to create)
2. Design professional cover/preview images
3. Write compelling product descriptions
4. Set up payment processing

### Week 4: Launch
1. List products on chosen platforms
2. Create social media presence
3. Reach out to potential customers/communities
4. Gather initial reviews and testimonials

### Month 2+: Scale
1. Create additional products based on sales data
2. Build email list for product launches
3. Consider bundle offerings for higher average order value
4. Expand to additional platforms
