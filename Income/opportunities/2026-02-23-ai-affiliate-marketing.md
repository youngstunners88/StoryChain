## Opportunity Overview

AI tools offer some of the highest affiliate commissions in tech. With recurring revenue models (SaaS) and high-ticket enterprise solutions, promoting AI products can generate significant passive income. The key is targeting the right audience with valuable content.

## Action Steps

### Week 1: Setup
1. [ ] Choose niche (AI writing tools, AI for business, AI for creators)
2. [ ] Sign up for 5-10 affiliate programs
3. [ ] Create content calendar (10 posts/month minimum)

### Week 2-4: Content Creation
4. [ ] Write 5 comparison articles ("Best AI Tools for X")
5. [ ] Create 5 tutorial videos
6. [ ] Build email list with free resource (e.g., "50 AI Prompts PDF")

### Month 2: Promotion
7. [ ] Publish 10 more pieces of content

9. [ ] Create affiliate landing pages

### Month 3+: Optimisation
10. [ ] Analyse which content drives most clicks
11. [ ] Double down on top performers
12. [ ] Add more affiliate programs
13. [ ] Build out email sequences

## Niche Strategies

### Strategy 1: AI Writing Tools Niche
- Focus: Bloggers, marketers, content creators
- Products: Jasper, Copy.ai, Writesonic, Frase
- Content: Writing tutorials, prompt guides, comparisons
- Estimated audience: 100K+ monthly searches

### Strategy 2: AI for Business Niche
- Focus: Small business owners, entrepreneurs
- Products: HubSpot, Notion, GoHighLevel, Salesforce Einstein
- Content: Business automation guides, ROI case studies
- Higher-ticket but smaller audience

### Strategy 3: AI for Creators Niche
- Focus: YouTubers, podcasters, social media
- Products: Descript, OpusClip, ElevenLabs, Runway
- Content: Video tutorials, workflow guides
- Highly engaged audience, good for video content

---

## Competition Analysis

**High Competition:**
- Many affiliate marketers in AI space
- Content quality and SEO determine winners
- Building trust is essential (disclose affiliations)

**Differentiation Strategies:**
- Focus on one sub-niche deeply
- Create original tutorials and use cases
- Build email list for direct promotion
- Offer bonuses for your affiliate links

---

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Low conversion rates | Optimise landing pages, add trust signals |
| Program changes/closures | Diversify across multiple programs |
| SEO algorithm changes | Build email list, diversify traffic sources |
| Competition | Focus on unique angles, better content |
