## Required Effort & Resources

### Initial Investment
- **Time:** 10-20 hours/week content creation
- **Money:** $100 - $500 (website, hosting, email marketing)
- **Skills:** Content creation, SEO basics, audience building

### Content Requirements
1. **Review Content:** Detailed AI tool reviews and comparisons
2. **Tutorial Content:** How-to guides for AI tools
3. **Case Studies:** Real results from using tools
4. **Comparison Articles:** "Tool A vs Tool B" content

### Platform Options
- Blog/website (SEO-focused)
- YouTube channel
- Newsletter
- Social media (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram)
- Niche communities (Reddit, Discord)

## Action Steps to Pursue

### Week 1-2: Setup
1. Choose 3-5 AI tools to focus on (match to target audience)
2. Sign up for affiliate programs (all free to join)
3. Create simple blog/website or social presence
4. Develop content strategy and editorial calendar

### Week 3-6: Content Creation
1. Write 10-15 in-depth tool reviews
2. Create comparison articles between tools
3. Develop tutorial content showing real use cases
4. Build email list with lead magnet (free AI tool guide)

### Week 7-12: Traffic & Optimization
1. Implement SEO optimization for all content
2. Promote content across social platforms
3. Engage in communities where target audience hangs out
4. Track conversions and optimize highest-performing content

### Month 3+: Scale
1. Expand to additional AI tools
2. Create video content for YouTube
3. Build partnerships with other creators
4. Consider paid promotion for top-converting content

## Best Strategies for Success

### Content Strategy
- **Comparison Content:** "Best AI Tools for [Specific Use Case]"
- **Tutorial Content:** Step-by-step guides
- **Honest Reviews:** Include pros AND cons (builds trust)
- **Update Regularly:** AI tools change fast

### Traffic Sources (Ranked by Effectiveness)
1. **SEO/Google Search:** Highest intent, converts well
2. **YouTube:** Great for tutorials, strong engagement
3. **Email Marketing:** Highest conversion rate
4. **Social Media:** Good for awareness, lower conversion
