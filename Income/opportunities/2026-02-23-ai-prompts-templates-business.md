# Opportunity: AI Prompts & Templates Business

**Type:** Digital Products / Passive Income  
**Date Identified:** 2026-02-23  
**Priority Score:** 7/10

---

## Overview

Create and sell AI prompts, templates, and digital products on platforms like Gumroad, Etsy, and Creative Market. "Create once, sell forever" model with minimal ongoing effort. Massive and growing demand from marketers, founders, and creators.

## Market Evidence

- Creators reporting **$11,200 in 6 months** from ChatGPT prompts[^1]
- AI-generated digital products trending on Etsy and Gumroad[^2]
- Demand for prompts from marketers, founders, creators growing[^3]
- Platforms: Gumroad, Etsy, Creative Market, PromptBase

## Potential Revenue Estimate

| Product Type | Price Point | Sales/Month | Monthly Revenue |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Prompt packs (5-10 prompts) | $5-15 | 50-100 | $250-1,500 |
| Notion templates | $15-49 | 30-50 | $450-2,450 |
| Canva templates | $10-25 | 40-80 | $400-2,000 |
| Template bundles | $29-99 | 20-40 | $580-3,960 |

**Combined portfolio potential:** $1,500-5,000/month

## Required Effort/Resources

**Initial Setup:**
- $0 to start (free Canva, ChatGPT access)
- 2-4 hours per product to create
- Account on selling platforms (free)

**Ongoing:**
- 2-3 hours/week for new products and marketing
- SEO optimization for marketplace visibility

## Product Ideas

### High-Demand Categories:

1. **Business Prompts**
   - Email marketing sequences
   - Content calendar templates
   - Business plan generators

2. **Creative Templates**
   - Social media content planners
   - Canva design templates
   - Notion productivity systems

3. **Niche Prompts**
   - Real estate listing descriptions
   - Legal document templates
   - Healthcare communication scripts

4. **Educational Products**
   - Course creation prompts
   - Training material templates
   - Workshop planners

## Action Steps

1. **Week 1:** Research top-selling prompts/templates on Gumroad and Etsy
2. **Week 2:** Create first 3 products in high-demand niches
3. **Week 3:** Launch on Gumroad (free), Etsy ($0.20/listing)
4. **Week 4:** Create social media presence, share free samples
5. **Month 2+:** Scale to 20+ products, create bundles

## Success Factors

- **Niche down:** Generic prompts don't sell; specific use cases do
- **Bundle strategically:** Increase average order value
- **Free samples:** Build trust with free mini-packs
- **SEO optimize:** Use keywords buyers search for
- **Update regularly:** Keep prompts current with AI model changes

## Why Medium-High Priority

- Very low barrier to entry ($0 start)
- True passive income after creation
- Can start immediately
- Scales with portfolio size
- No technical skills required

## Risks

- Market becoming saturated
- Need continuous product creation for growth
- Platform fees (Gumroad: 10%, Etsy: 6.5% + $0.20)
- AI model updates may make prompts obsolete
