## Opportunity Overview

Provide AI-powered booking agents and chatbots for local businesses (real estate, restaurants, wellness, home services). 83% of small businesses using AI report efficiency gains. Practitioners earn $300-500/month recurring per client with $300/day potential from 1-2 clients per month.

## Market Context

### AI Adoption in Small Business
- 83% of small business owners using AI report efficiency gains
- By 2026, 80% plan to integrate AI chatbots into customer support
- Small businesses save 20+ hours/month with AI tools
- Average savings: $500-2,000/month after AI adoption

### Specific Verticals

#### Real Estate
- Agents need 24/7 lead capture
- Booking property viewings is time-consuming
- Lead qualification is critical
- High ticket value = high willingness to pay

#### Restaurants
- 1 in 3 calls missed during business hours
- 80% of missed calls are booking-related
- Voice ordering is emerging trend
- Online ordering integration needed

#### Wellness Studios (Yoga, Pilates, Spas)
- Appointment scheduling is core business
- High no-show rates (revenue loss)
- Client communication is time-consuming
- Growing market with tech-savvy owners

#### Home Services (Plumbers, Electricians, Landscapers)
- Lead capture during after-hours is critical
- Scheduling coordination is complex
- Quote generation is time-consuming
- High average job value

## Specific Services by Vertical

### Real Estate Agents
- **Lead Capture:** 24/7 property enquiry bot
- **Qualification:** Automated questions (budget, timeline, location)
- **Booking:** Schedule property viewings automatically
- **Follow-up:** Automated nurture sequences
- **Pricing:** $500-750/month

### Restaurants
- **Reservation Bot:** Table booking on website/social
- **Voice Ordering:** AI-powered phone ordering
- **Menu Q&A:** Answer questions about menu, hours, location
- **Delivery Integration:** Connect to ordering platforms
- **Pricing:** $300-600/month

### Wellness Studios
- **Class Booking:** Schedule classes, manage capacity
- **Waitlist Management:** Automatic waitlist notifications
- **Membership Management:** Handle renewals, upgrades
- **Review Solicitation:** Automated review requests
- **Pricing:** $400-700/month

### Home Services
- **Emergency Dispatch:** Route after-hours calls appropriately
- **Quote Generation:** Preliminary estimates from descriptions
- **Scheduling:** Coordinate with technician calendars
- **Payment Processing:** Collect deposits automatically
- **Pricing:** $500-800/month
