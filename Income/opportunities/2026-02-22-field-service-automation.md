**Priority Score:** ⭐ 8/10  
**Status:** 🎯 UNDERSERVED MARKET - Low Competition, High Demand

---

## Opportunity Overview

Field service businesses (plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians, landscapers, etc.) represent a massive underserved market for AI and automation services. According to Salesforce's 2025 State of Service report, 80% of field service businesses are underserved by current technology solutions.

### Market Context
- 71% of service reps using AI say it's creating growth opportunities
- Field service businesses struggle with scheduling, dispatching, customer communication
- Low technology adoption creates huge opportunity gap
- High phone volume, emergency calls, complex scheduling needs
- Industry worth $500B+ in US alone
