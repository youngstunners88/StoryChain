## Opportunity Overview

AI receptionist and voice agent services are transforming how small businesses manage customer calls. This is a rapidly growing market with strong demand from small businesses seeking 24/7 availability at a fraction of traditional receptionist costs.

### Market Context
- AI receptionist tools can help businesses cut customer service costs by up to 32%
- 80% of small businesses plan to integrate AI chatbots into customer support by end of 2026
- Small businesses using AI receptionists report saving 20+ hours per month
- Market growing at 25-35% annually through 2028
