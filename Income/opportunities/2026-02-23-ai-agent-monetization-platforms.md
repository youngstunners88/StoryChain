## Pricing Strategies

### Usage-Based Pricing
- Charge per interaction or task
- Typical: $0.01-0.10 per interaction
- Works for high-volume agents

### Subscription Model
- Monthly access fee
- Typical: $9-99/month
- Predictable revenue, easier budgeting for users

### Outcome-Based Pricing
- Charge based on results delivered
- Higher risk/higher reward
- Examples: $10 per qualified lead, $50 per report

### Hybrid Model
- Base subscription + usage overages
- Platform fee + outcome bonuses
- Most sustainable for enterprise use
