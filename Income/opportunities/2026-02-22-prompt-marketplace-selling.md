## Opportunity Overview

Selling AI prompts on dedicated marketplaces has emerged as a legitimate income stream. While individual prompt sales are modest ($2-10 each), creators can build meaningful income through volume and quality.

### Market Context
- PromptBase, FlowGPT, PromptHero are leading marketplaces
- Top prompts sell for $2-50 each
- Premium packs and bundles command $20-50
- Realistic expectations: Most sellers earn modest supplemental income
- Some creators report consistent sales with quality prompts

## Quality Factors That Sell

### What Makes Prompts Sell

1. **Specificity** - Solves a specific problem
2. **Reliability** - Produces consistent results
3. **Documentation** - Clear usage instructions
4. **Examples** - Shows output quality
5. **Originality** - Not easily replicated

### Prompt Quality Checklist
- [ ] Clear, specific outcome
- [ ] Tested with multiple inputs
- [ ] Produces consistent results
- [ ] Includes usage examples
- [ ] Has unique value vs free prompts
- [ ] Well-documented

## Bottom Line

**Selling prompts can provide supplemental income** but should not be viewed as a primary income source. The most successful approach is to combine prompt sales with related offerings: prompt engineering services, AI consulting, or digital products like prompt guides and courses.

**Recommended Starting Point:** Create 20-30 high-quality prompts in a specific niche (e.g., business writing prompts or Midjourney style prompts). List on multiple platforms. Use prompt sales as a way to build authority and funnel clients to higher-value services.

**Realistic Expectation:** $100-500/month with consistent effort, potentially more if you build a brand around AI expertise.
