## Compliance & Best Practices

### Important Guidelines
1. **Always disclose** affiliate relationships
2. **Be honest** about pros and cons
3. **Don't overhype** - builds trust long-term
4. **Stay updated** on program terms
5. **Track everything** - conversions, clicks, earnings

### Red Flags to Avoid
- Programs with no payment history
- Unrealistic commission claims
- Poor or non-existent support
- Negative user reviews
