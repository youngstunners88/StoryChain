## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Commission changes | Diversify across 5-10 programs |
| Platform algorithm changes | Build email list as owned asset |
| Low conversion rates | Focus on high-intent keywords |
| Competition | Niche down, build trust |

---

## Key Success Factors

1. **Trust First** - Recommend only tools you've used
2. **Recurring > One-time** - Prioritize recurring commissions
3. **Content Consistency** - 3-5 pieces per week minimum
4. **Email List** - Capture leads for long-term value
5. **Niche Focus** - "AI for real estate" > "AI tools"
