## Opportunity Overview

Cryptocurrency exchange affiliate programs offer some of the highest commission rates in affiliate marketing—with some programs paying up to 50% recurring commissions on trading fees. With the crypto market maturing and stablecoin adoption growing ($300B market), this represents a scalable passive income opportunity.

### Market Context
- Binance offers 20-40% commission for top affiliates
- MEXC offers up to 50% commission on trading fees
- Crypto affiliate programs include two-tier structures (earn from sub-affiliate referrals)
- Recurring revenue model: earn every time your referral trades

## Two-Tier Opportunity

Many crypto exchanges offer two-tier affiliate programs:
- **Tier 1:** Direct referrals earn you commissions
- **Tier 2:** When your referrals refer others, you earn 10% of their commissions

**Strategy:** Create educational content teaching others how to become affiliates, generating Tier 2 income.
