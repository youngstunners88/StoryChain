## Opportunity Overview

AI Automation Agencies (AAA) are booming in 2026. Small businesses spend $200-500/month on AI tools but often don't know how to implement them. An AAA bridges this gap—implementing AI workflows, chatbots, and automation for monthly retainers of $300-1,000+ per client.

---

## Market Data

- **Small Business AI Spending:** $200-500/month average
- **ROI for Businesses:** 3-7x investment within 6 months
- **Agency Revenue Tiers:**
  - Local business automation: $300-500/month per client
  - Digital products: $5-50 per sale (unlimited volume)
  - Niche consulting: $150-500/hour
- **Practitioner Reports:** $300/day potential with 1-2 local clients/month

## Required Resources

### Skills Needed
- No-code automation platforms (Make, Zapier, n8n)
- Basic AI tool knowledge (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
- CRM systems (HubSpot, GoHighLevel)
- Client communication and sales

### Tools & Platforms
- **Make.com:** Workflow automation (free tier available)
- **GoHighLevel:** AI booking bots, lead gen ($97/month)
- **Zapier:** App integrations (free tier available)
- **n8n:** Self-hosted automation (free open source)
- **ChatGPT/Claude API:** AI capabilities

### Startup Costs
- Platform subscriptions: $50-200/month
- Website/branding: $100-300
- Marketing: $100-500/month
- Time investment: 15-30 hours/week

## Action Steps

### Week 1-2: Skill Building
1. [ ] Complete Make.com free courses
2. [ ] Learn GoHighLevel for booking bots
3. [ ] Build 3 demo automations for portfolio:
   - Lead capture → CRM → Email sequence
   - Appointment booking → Calendar → SMS reminder
   - Form submission → AI analysis → Slack notification

### Week 3-4: Positioning
4. [ ] Choose a niche (real estate, wellness, agencies, etc.)
5. [ ] Create service packages and pricing
6. [ ] Build simple website with case studies
7. [ ] Set up intake form for leads

### Month 2: Client Acquisition
8. [ ] Identify 50 businesses in chosen niche
9. [ ] Create personalised outreach (show demo for their business)
10. [ ] Offer free automation audit
11. [ ] Close first 3 clients at $500 setup + $300/month

### Month 3-6: Optimisation
12. [ ] Document all processes
13. [ ] Create templates for common automations
14. [ ] Build referral programme
15. [ ] Hire VA for repetitive tasks
