## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|------------|------------|
| Copycats/competitors | High | Build brand, create unique value |

## Success Factors

1. **Solve Real Problems** - Templates that save genuine time sell best
2. **Quality Over Quantity** - One excellent template beats ten mediocre ones
3. **Niche Down** - Specific templates outsell generic ones
4. **Visual Appeal** - Professional-looking templates command higher prices
5. **Clear Instructions** - Include usage guides and videos
