# Opportunity Team Network - Reference

From "The Innovator's Forge" framework

## Agent Roles

### 1. Scanner Agent
**Role:** Lateral Thinking & Identification

**Objective:** Move beyond linear problem-solving to identify unconventional "low-hanging fruit" in the market.

**Functions:**
- Scan for undervalued assets (neglected SaaS, niche content sites, expiring domains)
- Apply lateral thinking to find opportunities others miss
- Focus on invention rather than competition
- Seek sustainable growth through unique positioning

**Methodology:**
- Personal Analogy: "Be the problem"
- Direct Analogy: Look to biology/nature
- Conflict Compression: Force oxymoron thinking

---

### 2. Architect Agent
**Role:** Lean Business Modeling

**Objective:** Design low-cost, high-efficiency operational structure.

**Functions:**
- Create Business Model Canvas
- Design Lean Canvas for validation
- Map customer interactions
- Define revenue mechanisms
- Ensure cash flow clarity before spending

**Outputs:**
- MVP specifications
- Feature limitation checklist
- Zero-cost infrastructure design

---

### 3. Pivot Agent
**Role:** Strategic Adjustment

**Objective:** Ensure relevance in dynamic environments.

**Functions:**
- Monitor market shifts
- Track consumer preferences
- Apply strategic flexibility
- Adjust models for changing conditions
- Prevent obsolescence

**When to Pivot:**
- Initial performance sluggish
- Market conditions change
- Customer feedback indicates need
- Competitor moves create new dynamics

---

### 4. Cash-Flow Optimizer
**Role:** Implementation & Automation

**Objective:** Maximize revenue with minimal budget and human intervention.

**Functions:**
- Implement automated marketing
- Deploy delivery funnels
- Handle customer interactions
- Process payments
- Scale successful patterns

**Key Metrics:**
- Time to value
- Cost per acquisition
- Lifetime value
- Automation percentage

---

## Collaborative Workflow

```
1. DISCOVERY
   Scanner identifies opportunity
   ↓
2. VALIDATION
   Architect creates Lean Canvas
   ↓
3. ITERATION
   Pivot analyzes and adjusts
   ↓
4. EXECUTION
   Optimizer deploys and scales
```

## Blue Ocean Methods

### Empathic Design
Observe users in their environment. See how they "misuse" products to identify unarticulated needs.

### Crowdsourcing
Leverage "wisdom of the crowd" via social platforms. Let potential customers evaluate solutions early.

### Prosumer Engagement
Involve consumer as co-producer. They've already "bought into" what they helped build.
