## Why India Matters for AI

### Scale Advantage
With 1.4 billion people and a massive English-speaking workforce, India offers unmatched scale for AI training data, testing, and deployment. The country already produces 1.5 million engineering graduates annually.

### Cost Efficiency
AI development costs in India are 60-80% lower than the US or Europe. A prompt engineer in Bangalore earns $15,000-30,000 annually versus $80,000-150,000 in San Francisco.

### Regulatory Flexibility
India's approach to AI regulation has been more permissive than the EU's, creating a sandbox environment for rapid innovation.
