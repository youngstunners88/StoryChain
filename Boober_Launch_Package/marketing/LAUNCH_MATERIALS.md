### Full Description

Boober - Johannesburg's Premier Minibus Taxi Hailing App

The minibus taxi experience, reimagined for South Africa.

🚕 FIND TAXIS INSTANTLY
Discover nearby taxis and see their routes in real-time. No more standing at the rank wondering when the next taxi will arrive.

📱 TRACK YOUR RIDE
Watch your taxi approach on the live map. Share your journey with family and friends for added peace of mind.

💳 PAY YOUR WAY
Cash, card, or mobile payment - whatever works for you. Split fares with friends directly in the app.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 BUILT FOR COMMUNITY
Rate drivers, share feedback, and help build a safer, more reliable taxi network for everyone.

⭐ FOR DRIVERS
Join Johannesburg's growing network of verified taxi drivers. Get matched with passengers heading your way and increase your daily earnings.

🔒 SAFETY FIRST
- Real-time GPS tracking
- Emergency assistance button
- Driver verification

- Community reporting

🇿🇦 MADE FOR SOUTH AFRICA

Features:

## Target Audiences

**Primary: Daily Commuters**
- Age: 18-45
- Location: Johannesburg/Gauteng
- Income: R5,000 - R25,000/month
- Behaviour: Uses minibus taxis daily

**Secondary: Drivers**
- Age: 25-55
- Location: Johannesburg
- Requirements: PrDP, registered taxi
- Behaviour: Looking to increase earnings

**Tertiary: Safety-Conscious**
- Age: 18-35
- Behaviour: Values trip sharing and tracking
- Use case: Evening commutes, travel to unfamiliar areas
