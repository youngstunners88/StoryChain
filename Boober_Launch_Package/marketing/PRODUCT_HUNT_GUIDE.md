# Boober Product Hunt Launch Guide

## Pre-Launch Preparation

### 1. Build Your Presence (2-4 weeks before)
- [ ] Create Product Hunt account early
- [ ] Engage with other products in your category
- [ ] Connect with hunters (people who post products)
- [ ] Join relevant Product Hunt communities

### 2. Find a Hunter
A hunter is someone who posts your product. Having a well-known hunter can boost visibility.

**Top Hunters for Apps/Startups:**
- @chrismessina
- @kristoferthomas
- @bentossell
- @erictwillis

**How to Approach:**
1. Engage with their posts genuinely
2. Send a polite DM explaining your product
3. Offer early access
4. Don't spam - quality over quantity

### 3. Prepare Assets

**Required:**
- [ ] Product logo (220x220 PNG, no rounded corners)
- [ ] Hero image (1270x760 PNG)
- [ ] 5-8 product screenshots
- [ ] 60-second demo video (optional but recommended)
- [ ] Tagline (max 60 characters)

**Boober Assets:**
- Logo: Boober taxi icon
- Tagline: "Hail minibus taxis in Johannesburg"
- Hero: Show the app in action on a Johannesburg street
- Screenshots: Map view, ride tracking, payment, safety features

### 4. Write Your Product Description

**Tagline:** Hail minibus taxis in Johannesburg. Track rides, pay cashless, travel safely.

**Description:**

Boober - Johannesburg's Minibus Taxi Hailing App

The minibus taxi industry moves 15+ million South Africans daily. It's time technology served this vital sector.

**For Passengers:**
- Find nearby taxis in seconds
- Track your ride in real-time
- Pay with cash, card, or mobile
- Share your journey with loved ones
- Rate and review drivers

**For Drivers:**
- Get matched with passengers on your route
- Accept digital payments
- Build your reputation with ratings
- Lower commission than competitors

**Built for South Africa:**
- No surge pricing
- Works on any smartphone (PWA)
- Local language support
- Designed for township reality

We're not just another ride-sharing app. We're building technology that serves the real transport needs of South Africans.

Currently live in Johannesburg, expanding across Gauteng.

**Topics:** Transportation, Android, iPhone, PWA, South Africa

## Launch Day Strategy

### Timing
- **Best Day:** Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday
- **Best Time:** 12:01 AM Pacific Time (10:01 AM SAST)
- **Why:** You get maximum exposure during US business hours

### The Hunt (If Self-Hunting)
1. Go to producthunt.com/new
2. Fill in all details
3. Add makers (team members)
4. Set launch time to 12:01 AM PT
5. Publish and share!

### Launch Day Activities

**Hour 0-1 (12:01 AM - 1:00 AM PT)**
- [ ] Product goes live
- [ ] Share on Twitter immediately
- [ ] Post on LinkedIn
- [ ] Send email to subscribers
- [ ] Share in relevant Slack communities
- [ ] Post on Indie Hackers
- [ ] Share on Hacker News (if relevant)

**Hour 1-8 (1:00 AM - 8:00 AM PT)**
- [ ] Respond to every comment
- [ ] Thank upvoters
- [ ] Answer questions promptly
- [ ] Engage genuinely, don't just promote

**Hour 8-16 (8:00 AM - 4:00 PM PT)**
- [ ] Peak US traffic time
- [ ] Continue responding to comments
- [ ] Share updates on social
- [ ] Encourage team to engage

**Hour 16-24 (4:00 PM - 12:00 AM PT)**
- [ ] Keep responding
- [ ] Thank the community
- [ ] Monitor ranking
- [ ] Celebrate wins!

### Comment Response Templates

**For "How is this different from Uber/Bolt?"**
Great question! While Uber and Bolt focus on private car rides, Boober is specifically designed for the minibus taxi industry - the backbone of South African public transport. We're not replacing taxis, we're enhancing them. Drivers keep their autonomy, passengers get better visibility and safety, and we're working WITH the existing industry rather than disrupting it.

**For "What's the business model?"**
We take a small commission on rides (lower than traditional ride-sharing), and we're exploring additional revenue streams like in-app advertising, premium features for drivers, and partnerships with local businesses along popular routes.

**For "Why focus on minibus taxis?"**
The minibus taxi industry moves 15+ million South Africans every single day - that's 70% of public transport. Yet there's been almost no innovation in this space. We believe the people who need technology most are often the last to get it. We're changing that.

**For "How do you ensure driver quality?"**
We verify driver credentials (PrDP, vehicle registration, insurance), implement a rating system, and have a zero-tolerance policy for safety violations. We also work with taxi associations to onboard their verified members.

## Post-Launch

### 1. Thank Your Supporters
- Send personal thank-yous to top commenters
- Share results on social media
- Update your PH profile with the launch

### 2. Leverage the Badge
If you make Top 5, you get a badge:
- Add it to your website
- Include in marketing materials
- Share on social media

### 3. Use the Traffic
Product Hunt traffic has high bounce rates. Capture it:
- Have a clear call-to-action
- Offer something special to PH visitors
- Collect emails for updates

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Don't ask for upvotes directly - Against PH rules, can get penalised
- Don't use fake accounts - PH detects this easily
- Don't ignore comments - Engagement drives ranking
- Don't launch on weekends - Lower traffic
- Don't have a weak tagline - This is what people see first
- Don't forget screenshots - Visual products do better

---

## Best Practices

- Be genuine - Authentic engagement wins
- Respond quickly - First few hours are critical
- Show personality - People connect with stories
- Highlight impact - Show how you're helping real people
- Offer value - Share insights, not just promotion
- Build relationships - PH is a community, not just a platform

## Metrics to Track

- **Upvotes** (Primary ranking factor)
- **Comments** (Shows engagement)
- **Position** (Track throughout the day)
- **Referral traffic** (From PH to your site)
- **Sign-ups from PH** (Conversion tracking)
