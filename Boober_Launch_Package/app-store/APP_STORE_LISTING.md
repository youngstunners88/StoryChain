## App Store Optimization Notes

### Title Optimization
- Include primary keyword "Taxi"
- Keep brand name "Boober" prominent
- Stay under 30 characters

### Description Optimization
- First 3 lines critical for conversion
- Use bullet points for scanability
- Include social proof elements
- Mention key differentiators

### Keyword Strategy
Primary: taxi, minibus, Johannesburg, South Africa
Secondary: ride, hailing, transport, commute, track
Long-tail: township taxi, taxi app South Africa, Jozi taxi

### Screenshot Strategy
- First screenshot most important
- Show value proposition clearly
- Use captions to highlight features
- Show real app experience

- Recognisable taxi symbol
- Brand colour (#E63946)
- Clean, simple design
- Works at small sizes
