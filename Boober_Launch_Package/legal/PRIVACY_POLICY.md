# Boober Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Boober ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Boober mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Services").

Boober is a taxi-hailing and ride-sharing platform operating in South Africa, connecting passengers with taxi drivers and marshals for minibus taxi transportation.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Full name and surname
- Mobile phone number
- Email address
- Profile photograph (optional)
- South African ID number (for driver verification only)

**Payment Information:**
- Credit/debit card details (processed securely by third-party payment processors)
- Mobile payment account information
- Transaction history

**Ride Information:**
- Pickup and drop-off locations
- Ride routes and distances
- Timestamps of rides
- Fare amounts and payment methods used

**Driver-Specific Information (for driver accounts):**
- Professional Driving Permit (PrDP) number and expiry
- Vehicle registration and license disc details
- Vehicle make, model, and year
- Taxi association membership
- Insurance information

**Marshal-Specific Information (for marshal accounts):**
- Taxi rank assignment
- Association membership
- Authorisation credentials

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Location Information:**
- Real-time GPS location during active rides
- Device location when the app is in use
- Saved favourite locations (home, work, etc.)

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- Unique device identifiers
- IP address
- App version

**Usage Information:**
- App features used
- Pages viewed and actions taken
- Crash reports and error logs
- Performance metrics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

### For Passengers:
- Process ride requests and connect you with drivers
- Calculate and process fares
- Provide real-time ride tracking
- Send ride confirmations, receipts, and updates
- Enable safety features (ride sharing, emergency assistance)
- Personalise your experience
- Communicate about promotions and new features
- Respond to customer support requests
- Detect and prevent fraud

### For Drivers:
- Verify identity and driving credentials
- Match you with passenger requests
- Process payments and earnings
- Provide navigation assistance
- Send ride and payment notifications
- Maintain safety and quality standards
- Comply with regulatory requirements

### For All Users:
- Maintain and improve our Services
- Conduct research and analytics
- Ensure platform security
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes

## 4. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### With Other Users:
- **Passengers** can see driver name, photo, vehicle details, and real-time location during rides
- **Drivers** can see passenger name, pickup/drop-off locations, and contact number for active rides
- **Marshals** can see driver and passenger information for coordination purposes

### With Service Providers:
- Payment processors (for transaction processing)
- Cloud hosting providers (for app infrastructure)
- Analytics providers (for usage insights)
- Customer support platforms
- SMS and notification services

### For Legal Purposes:
- To comply with legal obligations
- To respond to lawful requests from public authorities
- To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- To enforce our agreements

### Business Transfers:
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, including:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest
- Secure authentication mechanisms
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and audit logs
- Staff training on data protection

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide Services. We may also retain information:

- To comply with legal obligations (e.g., financial records for 5 years per South African law)
- To resolve disputes and enforce agreements
- For legitimate business purposes with appropriate safeguards

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

## 7. Your Rights (POPIA Compliance)

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal information
- **Correction** of inaccurate information
- **Deletion** of your personal information
- **Object** to processing in certain circumstances
- **Data portability** in a structured format
- **Withdraw consent** at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@boober.co.za or submit a request through the App settings.

## 8. Cross-Border Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside South Africa. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

- Standard Contractual Clauses
- Binding Corporate Rules (where applicable)
- Consent where required

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Services are not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## 10. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## 11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following technologies:

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for the App to function
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how users interact with the App
- **Marketing Cookies:** Used to deliver relevant advertisements (with consent)

You can manage cookie preferences through the App settings or your device settings.

## 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:
- Posting the updated policy in the App
- Sending a push notification
- Email notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the Services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
