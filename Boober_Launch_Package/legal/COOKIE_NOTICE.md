# Boober Cookie Notice

**Last Updated: February 2025**

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our services.

## Types of Cookies We Use

Boober uses the following types of cookies and similar technologies:

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the app to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, secure login, and access to protected areas of the app. The app cannot function properly without these cookies.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| session_id | Maintains your logged-in session | Session |
| auth_token | Authenticates your identity | 7 days |
| csrf_token | Prevents cross-site request forgery | Session |
| language | Remembers your language preference | 1 year |

### Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalisation, such as remembering your preferences and providing improved features.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| last_location | Remembers your last used location | 30 days |
| saved_places | Stores your favourite locations | 1 year |
| payment_method | Remembers your preferred payment method | 6 months |
| theme | Stores your display preferences | 1 year |

### Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our app by collecting anonymous statistical data. This information helps us improve our services.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| _ga | Google Analytics - distinguishes users | 2 years |
| _ga_* | Google Analytics - maintains session state | 2 years |
| amplitude_id | Amplitude analytics - user identification | 1 year |
| _fbp | Facebook Pixel - tracking for analytics | 3 months |

### Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. They may be set by our advertising partners.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| _fbc | Facebook Click ID | 3 months |
| google_ads_id | Google Ads conversion tracking | 90 days |
| twitter_ads_id | Twitter ads tracking | 30 days |

## How to Manage Cookies

### In the App

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time:
1. Go to Settings
2. Tap on Privacy
3. Select Cookie Preferences
4. Toggle the categories you wish to enable/disable

### In Your Browser

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the app.
