## iHhashi - Delivery Platform

### Overview
iHhashi is a **delivery platform** (NOT taxi/ride-hailing) for:
- Groceries (supermarkets, spaza shops)
- Food (restaurants, takeaways)
- Fruits & Vegetables
- Dairy Products
- Personal Courier Services

### Key Achievements

- ✅ Frontend UI development in progress
- ✅ Android debug APK built (3.6MB)
- ✅ Signed release APK ready (2.9MB)
- ✅ Play Store screenshots ready

- ✅ Privacy policy published at https://kofi.zo.space/privacy-policy

### Platform Architecture
- **Three-sided marketplace**: Customers, Merchants, Riders
- **Vendor types**: Restaurants, food vendors, fruits & vegetables, groceries
- **Market**: South Africa (all 9 provinces)
- **Currency**: ZAR (R)
