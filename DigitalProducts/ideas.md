# Digital Product Ideas - February 23, 2026

## 1. Vibe Coding Prompt Library

### Product Description
A comprehensive collection of tested, optimised prompts for building business applications with vibe coding platforms like Emergent, Cursor, and Lovable. Each prompt includes variations, expected outputs, and customisation guidelines.

### Market Size
- **TAM (Total Addressable Market)**: $2.1B (No-code/low-code market projected 2026)
- **SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)**: $420M (AI-assisted development tools)
- **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)**: $4.2M (1% of SAM)

### Target Audience
- Small business owners wanting custom tools
- Freelancers offering rapid prototyping services
- Non-technical founders building MVPs
- Enterprise teams prototyping internal tools
- Students and educators in digital skills programmes

### Monetisation Strategy
| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Starter Pack | $29 | 50 core prompts, basic templates |
| Professional | $79 | 200 prompts, customisation guide, examples |
| Enterprise | $199 | Full library, priority updates, team license |

**Revenue Projection:**
- Month 1-3: 50 sales @ $49 avg = $2,450
- Month 4-6: 150 sales @ $59 avg = $8,850
- Month 7-12: 300 sales @ $69 avg = $20,700
- **Year 1 Total: ~$32,000**

### Distribution Channels
- Gumroad (direct sales)
- Product Hunt launch
- Twitter/X content marketing
- LinkedIn articles
- Partnership with vibe coding platforms

### Competitive Advantage
- Curated, tested prompts (not AI-generated lists)
- Business-focused applications (not toy examples)
- Regular updates as platforms evolve
- Community feedback integration
