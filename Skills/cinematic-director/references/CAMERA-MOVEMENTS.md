# Camera Movements Reference

A comprehensive guide to camera movements for cinematic AI video generation.

## Static Shots

### Locked Off
- **Effect:** Complete stillness, control, timelessness
- **Use:** Establishing shots, formal portraits, moments of realization
- **Prompt:** "Camera static, locked off, no movement"

### Subtle Breath
- **Effect:** Organic feel, human presence
- **Use:** Intimate moments, subtle tension
- **Prompt:** "Camera with subtle breathing movement, almost imperceptible sway"

## Panning Movements

### Pan Left/Right
- **Effect:** Discovery, reveal, searching
- **Use:** Revealing environment, following eyeline, exploration
- **Prompt:** "Camera pans [left/right] revealing [subject/environment]"
- **Speed:** Slow (3-5 sec) for dramatic reveals, fast (1-2 sec) for urgency

### Whip Pan
- **Effect:** Disorientation, energy, time passage
- **Use:** Action sequences, scene transitions, chaos
- **Prompt:** "Whip pan [left/right] to [subject]"

### Pan Following Subject
- **Effect:** Connection, tracking
- **Use:** Following character movement
- **Prompt:** "Camera pans to follow [subject] as they [action]"

## Tilting Movements

### Tilt Up
- **Effect:** Revelation, grandeur, rising status
- **Use:** Revealing tall structures, character empowerment
- **Prompt:** "Camera tilts up from [low element] to [high element]"
- **Emotional beat:** From ground to face = rising hope, strength

### Tilt Down
- **Effect:** Diminishment, revelation from above
- **Use:** Revealing ground-level elements, character defeat
- **Prompt:** "Camera tilts down from [high element] to [low element]"
- **Emotional beat:** From face to ground = falling, defeat

### Tilt Reveal
- **Effect:** Surprise, context
- **Use:** Revealing full character or environment
- **Prompt:** "Camera tilts to reveal [subject/environment]"

## Zoom Movements

### Slow Push In (Dolly In Feel)
- **Effect:** Intimacy, focus, building tension
- **Use:** Emotional moments, character focus, suspense
- **Prompt:** "Camera slowly pushes in on [subject]"
- **Timing:** 4-6 seconds for emotional weight

### Fast Push In
- **Effect:** Shock, urgency, impact
- **Use:** Jump scares, realizations, action
- **Prompt:** "Camera quickly pushes in on [subject]"
- **Timing:** 0.5-1 second

### Slow Pull Back (Dolly Out Feel)
- **Effect:** Context, isolation, revelation
- **Use:** Revealing environment, showing isolation
- **Prompt:** "Camera slowly pulls back revealing [context]"
- **Timing:** 4-6 seconds for dramatic effect

### Crash Zoom
- **Effect:** Style, emphasis, grindhouse feel
- **Use:** Stylized moments, emphasis
- **Prompt:** "Crash zoom into [subject]"

## Dolly/Tracking Movements

### Dolly Forward
- **Effect:** Immersion, entering space
- **Use:** Moving into scenes, approaching characters
- **Prompt:** "Camera dollies forward into [space/scene]"

### Dolly Backward
- **Effect:** Leaving, distance, context
- **Use:** Exiting scenes, revealing scale
- **Prompt:** "Camera dollies backward away from [subject]"

### Tracking Shot (Parallel)
- **Effect:** Journey, movement, companionship
- **Use:** Following walking characters, side-by-side movement
- **Prompt:** "Camera tracks alongside [subject] as they [action]"

### Tracking Shot (Following)
- **Effect:** Pursuit, following, tension
- **Use:** Following from behind, chase scenes
- **Prompt:** "Camera follows behind [subject] as they [action]"

## Crane/Aerial Movements

### Crane Up
- **Effect:** Grandeur, scale, rising perspective
- **Use:** Revealing landscapes, cityscapes, scale
- **Prompt:** "Camera rises up revealing [vast environment]"

### Crane Down
- **Effect:** Grounding, intimacy, descending
- **Use:** Bringing focus to ground-level action
- **Prompt:** "Camera descends from above to [subject]"

### Bird's Eye
- **Effect:** Omniscient, detached, strategic
- **Use:** Tactical view, showing layout
- **Prompt:** "Camera in bird's eye view looking down on [scene]"

## Handheld Movements

### Steady Handheld
- **Effect:** Documentary feel, authenticity
- **Use:** Realistic scenes, interview-style
- **Prompt:** "Steady handheld camera, documentary feel, slight organic movement"

### Shaky Handheld
- **Effect:** Chaos, danger, urgency
- **Use:** Action, fear, disorientation
- **Prompt:** "Shaky handheld camera, urgent, following chaos"

### POV Handheld
- **Effect:** First-person, immersion
- **Use:** Character's perspective, horror
- **Prompt:** "POV handheld camera, as if from [character's] eyes"

## Combination Movements

### Push In + Tilt Up
- **Effect:** Rising revelation
- **Use:** Character empowerment
- **Prompt:** "Camera pushes in while tilting up on [subject]"

### Pull Back + Pan
- **Effect:** Expanding context
- **Use:** Revealing larger picture
- **Prompt:** "Camera pulls back and pans to reveal [environment]"

### Dolly + Pan
- **Effect:** Dynamic reveal
- **Use:** Moving around subjects
- **Prompt:** "Camera dollies [direction] while panning to keep [subject] in frame"

## Movement Speed Guide

| Speed | Duration | Effect | Use |
|-------|----------|--------|-----|
| Very Slow | 6-10 sec | Contemplative, heavy | Emotional climaxes |
| Slow | 4-6 sec | Dramatic, meaningful | Key moments |
| Medium | 2-4 sec | Standard, natural | General use |
| Fast | 1-2 sec | Urgent, energetic | Action, surprise |
| Very Fast | 0.5-1 sec | Shock, impact | Jump cuts, emphasis |

## Movement Choice Matrix

| Emotion | Primary Movement | Secondary Option |
|---------|------------------|------------------|
| Power | Low angle static | Low angle slow push in |
| Vulnerability | High angle pull back | High angle static |
| Tension | Off-center static | Slow pan |
| Intimacy | Close-up slow push in | Medium static |
| Chaos | Handheld tracking | Shaky zoom |
| Hope | Low angle tilt up | Push in to face |
| Defeat | High angle tilt down | Pull back |
| Discovery | Pan reveal | Tilt reveal |
| Isolation | Wide pull back | High angle static |
| Determination | Close-up push in | Low angle static |

## Pro Tips

1. **Less is more** - One clear movement per shot
2. **Match movement to emotion** - Never random
3. **Consider sound** - Movement suggests audio rhythm
4. **Think transitions** - How does this shot flow to the next?
5. **Test speed** - Generate at multiple durations to find the right feel
