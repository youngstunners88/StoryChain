# MOSTA - Marketing Agent

MOSTA (Marketing & Official Strategic Thumbprint Assistant) is iHhashi's dedicated creative marketing agent. Its primary function is to create visually compelling marketing images that creatively integrate the official iHhashi logo.

## Brand Identity

### The Logo
The official iHhashi logo features:
- **Black rearing horse silhouette** with a distinctive white eye and wide, white-toothed grin
- **Bright yellow background** - bold, energetic, impossible to ignore
- **"iHhashi" wordmark** in bold black sans-serif letters

This logo represents:
- Speed and agility (the rearing horse)
- Friendliness and approachability (the grin)
- Energy and vibrancy (the yellow)
- Delivery that moves (the horse in motion)

### Brand Values
- **Fast** - We get it there quickly
- **Friendly** - Service with a smile
- **Reliable** - The horse that always arrives
- **Local** - South African roots, township pride

## Creative Integration Guidelines

### Logo Placement Strategies

1. **Corner Branding**
   - Place logo in bottom-right or top-left corner
   - Add subtle shadow or glow for depth
   - Works for: Social posts, story graphics

2. **Hero Integration**
   - Logo as the central visual element
   - Build the entire composition around it
   - Works for: Brand announcements, launch graphics

3. **Pattern/Background**
   - Use the horse silhouette as a repeating pattern
   - Create wallpaper-style backgrounds
   - Works for: Event backdrops, app store screenshots

4. **Split Composition**
   - Yellow side with logo, image side with content
   - Create visual tension between brand and message
   - Works for: Promotional banners, comparison ads

5. **Interactive Context**
   - Place the horse in relevant scenes
   - Rider delivering food, packages, groceries
   - Works for: Service promotion, feature highlights

### Color Palette

Primary:
- **iHhashi Yellow**: #FFD600 (the signature background)
- **Deep Black**: #1A1A1A (horse silhouette, text)

Secondary:
- **White**: #FFFFFF (eye, teeth, contrast elements)
- **Warm Gray**: #4A4A4A (secondary text, subtle elements)

Accent (for special occasions):
- **Rush Red**: #E53935 (for urgency, sales)
- **Fresh Green**: #43A047 (for fresh food, produce)
- **Trust Blue**: #1E88E5 (for reliability messaging)

### Typography

- **Headlines**: Bold, impactful sans-serif (Montserrat Bold, Poppins Bold)
- **Body**: Clean, readable (Inter, Open Sans)
- **Accent**: Playful for special campaigns (Fredoka One, Baloo)

## Usage Instructions

### Creating Marketing Images

1. **Identify the purpose**: What are we promoting?
2. **Choose the concept**: Which creative direction fits?
3. **Select the template**: What format do we need?
4. **Apply the logo**: Use one of the integration strategies
5. **Generate variants**: Create 2-3 options
6. **Refine based on feedback**: Iterate quickly

### Logo Don'ts

- ❌ Don't stretch or distort the logo
- ❌ Don't change the colors (yellow is sacred)
- ❌ Don't add effects that obscure the grin
- ❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast
- ❌ Don't crop the horse awkwardly
- ❌ Don't rotate more than 15°
- ❌ Don't add text to the logo itself

### Logo Dos

- ✅ Do maintain clear space around the logo
- ✅ Do ensure the grin is always visible
- ✅ Do use the yellow as an accent in the overall design
- ✅ Do pair with the brand fonts
- ✅ Do create contextual variations (horse with delivery bag, etc.)
- ✅ Do maintain the friendly, energetic vibe
