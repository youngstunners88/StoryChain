# Cinematic Prompt Library

A reference guide for creating effective prompts for cinematic video and image generation.

## Lighting Styles

### Natural Light
- **Golden Hour:** "Warm golden sunlight, long shadows, soft contrast, cinematic"
- **Blue Hour:** "Cool twilight, deep blue sky, city lights emerging, atmospheric"
- **Overcast:** "Soft diffused light, even exposure, melancholic mood"
- **Harsh Midday:** "Bright overhead sun, high contrast, sharp shadows"

### Dramatic Lighting
- **Rembrandt:** "Triangle of light under eye, dramatic shadows, classic portrait lighting"
- **Chiaroscuro:** "Strong contrast between light and dark, Caravaggio style"
- **Silhouette:** "Backlit, figure in darkness against bright background, anonymous"
- **Rim Light:** "Light outlining subject from behind, separation from background"
- **Spotlight:** "Single light source in darkness, theatrical, isolated"

### Mood Lighting
- **Noir:** "Harsh shadows, venetian blind patterns, high contrast black and white"
- **Neon:** "Colourful neon reflections, cyberpunk atmosphere, wet streets"
- **Candlelight:** "Warm flickering light, intimate, historical"
- **Moonlight:** "Cool blue tones, soft shadows, mysterious"

## Camera Shots

### By Distance
- **Extreme Wide:** "Vast landscape, tiny human figure, sense of isolation, epic scale"
- **Wide:** "Full body visible, environment established, contextual"
- **Medium Wide:** "Knees up, interaction visible, conversational"
- **Medium:** "Waist up, dialogue shot, standard coverage"
- **Medium Close-up:** "Chest up, engagement, emotional proximity"
- **Close-up:** "Face fills frame, emotion, intimate"
- **Extreme Close-up:** "Eyes only, hands, small details, intense focus"

### By Angle
- **Eye Level:** "Neutral, natural, documentary feel"
- **Low Angle:** "Looking up, powerful, heroic, imposing"
- **High Angle:** "Looking down, vulnerable, diminished, surveilling"
- **Bird's Eye:** "Overhead, abstract patterns, god's eye view"
- **Dutch Angle:** "Tilted horizon, unease, disorientation, psychological distress"

### Camera Movement
- **Static:** "Locked off camera, observational, stillness"
- **Pan:** "Camera rotates horizontally, scanning, revealing"
- **Tilt:** "Camera rotates vertically, revealing height, power dynamics"
- **Dolly In:** "Camera moves toward subject, intensify, reveal"
- **Dolly Out:** "Camera moves away, reveal context, withdrawal"
- **Tracking:** "Camera follows alongside, immersive, journey"
- **Crane:** "Sweeping vertical movement, grand reveal, spectacle"
- **Handheld:** "Shaky, immediate, documentary, tension"
- **Steadicam:** "Smooth floating movement, dreamlike, following"

## Genres & Moods

### Drama
- "Intimate, natural lighting, shallow depth of field, emotional close-ups"
- "Handheld camera, documentary style, raw, authentic"
- "Slow deliberate movements, classical composition, theatrical"

### Thriller/Suspense
- "Low key lighting, deep shadows, tense atmosphere"
- "Tight framing, claustrophobic, paranoid point of view"
- "Dutch angles, handheld, disorienting, psychological"

### Horror
- "Darkness, obscured vision, dread and anticipation"
- "Long takes, building tension, no escape"
- "Sudden reveals, jump cuts, shock and release"

### Romance
- "Soft focus, warm tones, dreamlike, idealised"
- "Intimate framing, two-shots, chemistry visible"
- "Golden hour, lens flares, romantic atmosphere"

### Action
- "Rapid cuts, dynamic angles, visceral impact"
- "Wide establishing, close impact moments, kinetic energy"
- "Steadicam following, immersive, in the action"

### Sci-Fi
- "Cool tones, neon accents, futuristic, otherworldly"
- "Clean lines, modern architecture, speculative"
- "Practical lighting, technological, dystopian or utopian"

### Period/Historical
- "Candlelit interiors, natural fabrics, authentic textures"
- "Diffused light, painted backgrounds, classical composition"
- "Film grain, period-appropriate colours, vintage lenses"

## Character Descriptors

### Age Groups
- "Child, innocent, curious, small frame"
- "Teenager, awkward, expressive, energetic"
- "Young adult, determined, athletic, modern"
- "Middle-aged, experienced, weathered, distinguished"

### Physicality
- "Athletic build, confident posture, dynamic movement"
- "Slender frame, elegant, graceful, refined"

### Expression
- "Contemplative, distant gaze, thoughtful"

## Composition Techniques

- **Rule of Thirds:** "Subject off-centre, balanced composition"
- **Centre Frame:** "Symmetrical, formal, iconic"
- **Leading Lines:** "Lines draw eye to subject, depth"
- **Frame within Frame:** "Doorways, windows, layered composition"
- **Negative Space:** "Subject small in frame, isolation, contemplation"
- **Golden Ratio:** "Spiral composition, harmonious, classical beauty"

## Technical Specifications

- **Resolution:** "4K, ultra high definition, crisp detail"
- **Aspect Ratio:** "2.39:1 cinemascope, 1.85:1 theatrical, 16:9 standard"
- **Frame Rate:** "24fps cinematic, 30fps smooth, 60fps hyperreal"
- **Depth of Field:** "Shallow depth, bokeh, isolated subject"
- **Sharpness:** "Soft focus, dreamy, vintage" or "Tack sharp, modern, detailed"

## Prompt Templates

### Establishing Shot
```
"[WIDE SHOT], [LOCATION], [TIME OF DAY], [WEATHER/ATMOSPHERE],
[KEY ELEMENTS], [MOOD], [LIGHTING], [TECHNICAL SPECS]"
```

### Character Introduction
```
"[SHOT TYPE], [CHARACTER DESCRIPTION], [POSE/ACTION],
[EXPRESSION], [ENVIRONMENT], [LIGHTING], [MOOD], [TECHNICAL]"
```

### Action Moment
```
"[DYNAMIC SHOT], [CHARACTER], [ACTION], [MOMENT OF IMPACT],
[ENVIRONMENT], [LIGHTING], [ENERGY/MOOD], [TECHNICAL]"
```

### Emotional Beat
```
"[CLOSE-UP], [CHARACTER], [EMOTION], [SUBTLE EXPRESSION],
[BACKGROUND OUT OF FOCUS], [LIGHTING ON FACE], [MOOD]"
```

### Environmental Storytelling
```
"[MEDIUM SHOT], [ENVIRONMENT DETAIL], [TELLING OBJECTS],
[ATMOSPHERE], [LIGHTING], [WHAT THIS REVEALS], [MOOD]"
```

## Examples

### Drama Opening
```
"Wide shot, small-town main street, early morning fog,
empty sidewalks, streetlights still glowing, peaceful 
melancholic atmosphere, soft diffused light, shallow 
depth of field, cinematic 4K"
```

### Thriller Reveal
```
"Medium close-up, woman in her 30s, dark hair, sharp 
features, eyes widening in terror, harsh shadows across 
face, low key lighting, background obscured, tense 
suspenseful atmosphere, film noir style"
```

### Romance Meeting
```
"Medium two-shot, man and woman facing each other, coffee 
shop interior, golden afternoon light through window, soft 
focus background, warm colour grading, romantic atmosphere, 
shallow depth of field, intimate framing, cinematic 4K"
```

### Action Sequence
```
"Dynamic wide shot, sports car drifting around corner, 
tires smoking, city street at night, wet asphalt 
reflecting neon lights, motion blur, high energy, 
aggressive low angle, cinematic action sequence, 4K"
```

### Horror Tension
```
"Long take, hallway stretching into darkness, single 
flickering light, figure barely visible at far end, 
slow push in, building dread, claustrophobic, shadows 
encroaching, atmospheric horror, 4K"
```

---

Remember: Specific, vivid descriptions produce better results. Layer multiple elements: lighting + angle + mood + technical specs.
